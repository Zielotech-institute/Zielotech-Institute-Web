About The Program

Data analytics is the process of examining and analyzing raw data to extract meaningful insights, patterns, and trends. It involves applying various statistical and computational techniques to large datasets to uncover valuable information and support decision making. The goal of data analytics is to discover useful and actionable insights that can be used to improve business operations, optimize strategies, and gain a competitive advantage.
