About The Program

A data scientist is a professional who uses their expertise in mathematics, statistics, computer science, and domain knowledge to extract insights and knowledge from large and complex datasets. They employ various analytical techniques, programming skills, and machine learning algorithms to analyze data, identify patterns, and solve complex problems.
